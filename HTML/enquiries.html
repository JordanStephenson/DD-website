When enquiring about a tattoo please ensure you have included the following:

- Size (in cm's) and placement (where on your body you would like the tattoo)

- Any images that you feel will help with the designing of your tattoo.

- Preferred date for a consultation/tattoo booking.

- Preferred Artist
