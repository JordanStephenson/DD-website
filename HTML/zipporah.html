Zipporah Hunt

Zipporah started as an apprentice with us in May 2019. She has a love of face painting and body art, which has translated really well into tattooing. She has a great eye for patterns and symetry, which shines through in her intricate designs. She specialises in creating floral and astrological tattoos and has a high level of expertise in this area.
